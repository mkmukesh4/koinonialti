About us

Dr. Devasahayam’s vision and leadership led to the establishment of Koinonia Leadership Training Institute in the year 2020 to train ministers of God for various types of ministries. KLTI’s graduates are now serving as evangelists, pastors, and cross-cultural ministers around the world. KLTI strives to encourage one another in the knowledge of Christ and the pursuit of truth. We also want students to develop caring relationships in their community. Spiritual development and emotional maturity are emphasised as ways to prepare students for future ministry.

We offer four programs up to the post-graduate level. We have trained over 1000 plus church leaders, pastors, missionaries, evangelists, and lay people belonging to various denominations and institutions for over 10 years.

COURSES offered

The Koinonia Leadership Training Institute offers a variety of streams for students to pursue their theological education. The courses are offered by Asian Theological Association-accredited programmes (ATA). The goal of these programmes is to provide believers with solid biblical and theological knowledge so that they can join God's mission as partners. The courses designed will assist students in becoming scripturally and academically sound, ministry-oriented, and Holy Spirit-powered.
